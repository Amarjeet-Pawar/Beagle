The Beagle Blog

The Beagle is a small to medium-sized dog breed known for its friendly nature and excellent sense of smell. It was originally bred in England for hunting small animals like rabbits and hares. Beagles are popular family pets because they are gentle, playful, and good with children. They usually have short fur, long floppy ears, and a strong nose, which makes them one of the best scent hounds. Beagles are also very active and curious, so they need regular exercise and attention.
